Project 2

Farewell to my Specialty: A Poetical Compilation!

BESP

We wrote a poem about our time in the specialty from 10th to 12th grade. We included our experiences, lessons, and important moments. We used simple poetic devices like metaphors and similes to make the poem more interesting. Then, we decorated the poster with a lot of drawings. It was a chance to remember how much we have grown and learned. We also used technical vocabulary related to our specialty to include in the poem. We felt proud to share our work with the teacher. This project helped us reflect on our time in the specialty.

⟡ Reflection ⟡

1. What difficulties or obstacles did you experience while working on this project?

I found it challenging to choose the right words to express my journey in a poetic way and keep the rhythm consistent.

2. What skills did you apply during this project?

I applied my writing skills, used poetic devices like metaphors and similes, and practiced organizing my ideas clearly and creatively.

3. Did you develop or improve any new skills? If so, how?

Yes, I improved my ability to use figurative language to convey emotions and create more vivid imagery.

4. How could you improve this project if given more time or resources?

With more time, I would work on refining the structure of the poem and adding stronger, more impactful visual elements.
