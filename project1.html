Project 1

Current Events in Software Development: The Good and the Bad

BESP

In this group project, we researched two recent news articles related to software development. One article showed a positive event: AI tools improving web accessibility for people with disabilities. The second article was negative: a major data breach at a tech company that exposed millions of users’ information. We summarized both articles and created an infographic with clear visuals and key facts. The goal was to understand how real-world news affects our field and what we can learn from it as future professionals.

⟡ Reflection ⟡

1. What difficulties or obstacles did you experience while working on this project?

It was hard to find updated and reliable articles. Summarizing them clearly and organizing the infographic design was also a challenge.

2. What skills did you apply during this project?

We used research, reading, and writing skills in English. We also applied digital design skills to build the infographic..

3. Did you develop or improve any new skills? If so, how?

Yes, I improved my ability to identify trustworthy sources and present information in a visual format that others can understand easily.

4. How could you improve this project if given more time or resources?

I would improve the infographic layout and add more visuals or real quotes from experts in the field.
